Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-10

Helping Robots Inc. (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides MomRadar, an AI-enabled assistive mobile application designed to help caregivers notice potentially dangerous situations around kids by analyzing camera imagery and sending alerts.

1) What information we may collect

Camera imagery, device and app data, notifications data, and contact information only if you choose to provide it.

2) How we use information

To provide hazard detection and alert notifications, maintain and improve app performance, and communicate with you if you request it.

3) AI processing and third parties

MomRadar may use third-party AI/vision providers to process images for hazard detection. When enabled, imagery may be transmitted to such providers to perform the analysis.

4) Data retention

We aim to minimize retention. Any stored data is kept only as long as needed unless a longer period is required by law or you explicitly consent.

5) Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6) Children’s privacy

MomRadar is intended for use by caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children directly.

7) Your choices

You can disable camera access in device settings, request deletion of certain data where applicable, and opt out of marketing communications.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time.
