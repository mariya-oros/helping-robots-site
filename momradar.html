MomRadar

Notice danger sooner. Get alerted fast.

MomRadar is a mobile app that helps caregivers notice potentially dangerous situations around kids using AI vision models. When potential danger is detected, MomRadar can notify caregivers so they can act quickly.

How it works

A simple flow designed for caregivers.

1

Observe the environment

The app uses the device camera or configured captures to understand what’s happening around the child.

2

AI checks for potential hazards

Vision models analyze the scene for patterns that may indicate risk.

3

Alert when attention is needed

When something looks unsafe, MomRadar can notify caregivers so they can intervene.

4

Improve over time

Feedback and model tuning help reduce false alerts and improve usefulness, with privacy safeguards.

Common use cases

Examples of moments where caregivers may want extra awareness.

🪜

Climbing / falling risk

Detect scenarios where a child is climbing onto furniture or near ledges.

🔥

Heat / fire sources

Flag proximity to stoves, candles, heaters, or other hot surfaces.

⚠️

Sharp / dangerous objects

Identify possible presence of knives, tools, chemicals, or other hazards.
