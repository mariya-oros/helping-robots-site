The Challenge

Protective supervision remains almost entirely manual.

Families providing protective supervision balance constant awareness with everyday life, yet few tools have been designed specifically for these unique challenges.

Helping Robots believes families deserve technology built specifically for protective supervision—not adapted from security systems or general monitoring solutions.

Why Helping Robots

Purpose-built technology for an overlooked caregiving challenge.

Protective supervision is one of the most important—and least supported—challenges in caregiving.

Helping Robots exists to build technology designed specifically for these needs, combining thoughtful AI with real-world caregiving insight to create tools that support families, not replace them.

Our Approach to Safety

Technology should make caregiving more manageable—not more complicated. Every decision we make is guided by four principles.

🤝

Human support over automation

Technology should assist caregivers—not replace the judgment, intuition, and relationships that make care possible.

⚡

Alerts only when they matter

Caregivers already manage constant demands. Our goal is meaningful awareness, not unnecessary interruptions.

🔒

Privacy before convenience

Families deserve transparency and control over how their information is used and protected.

👨‍👩‍👧

Designed with families

The people closest to the challenge should help shape the technology designed to support them.

Our Vision

Supporting protective supervision wherever care takes place.

Helping Robots was founded with a long-term vision: to build technology that supports protective supervision across the people and settings involved in care.

Families

Continue developing tools that make everyday protective supervision safer and more manageable.

Professionals

Expand tools that strengthen communication, awareness, and collaboration between caregivers and care providers.

Organizations

Build solutions that help organizations better support safety, independence, and quality of care at scale.
