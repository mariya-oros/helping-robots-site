Our Story

Helping Robots started with our family.

For years, we've cared for two children who require protective supervision. We know what it's like to always be listening, always watching, and always thinking one step ahead. We also know how mentally exhausting that responsibility can be.

As we searched for tools that could actually help, we realized they didn't exist. Most technology was built for security, not for families like ours.

Helping Robots was created to support caregivers, reduce caregiver stress, improve safety, and create more opportunities for safe independence.

This is more than the work we do. It's part of our everyday life.
